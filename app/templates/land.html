Welcome to our Flask App

How to use our Flask App

- Click on the 'Home' tab to see the top news stories.

- Click on the 'About' tab to learn more about us and our website.

- Click on the 'Login/Logout' tab to login or logout of your account.

- If you do not have an account, click on the 'Login' tab to sign up.

- Once logged into your account, click on the 'Profile' tab to see which news posts you have liked and disliked.

- If you are logged as an admin, click the 'Admin' tab to see the news posts liked or disliked by all users.
